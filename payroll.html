Payroll FAQs

Quick answers to questions businesses ask before switching to managed payroll

How many employees can you handle? +

We handle payroll for businesses with 1 employee to 5000+ employees. Our process is fully scalable — pricing adjusts based on headcount with no setup fees.

Can you integrate with our existing HR software? +

Yes. We work with data from most HR and attendance systems including Excel, greytHR, Darwin Box, Keka and others. Data migration is handled by our team.

What statutory compliances do you cover? +

We cover PF (EPF/VPF), ESI, Professional Tax, TDS (Form 24Q), Labour Welfare Fund and Form 16 — all included in our managed payroll service.

How do employees receive their payslips? +

Password-protected payslips are delivered directly to employee emails. We can also provide bulk downloads for your HR team or integrate with your existing employee portal.
